Why It Matters

Federal Court Is Not
State Court. Not Even Close.

Federal criminal cases move faster, carry heavier penalties, and are prosecuted by attorneys who do nothing but federal criminal work. The conviction rate in federal court exceeds 90 percent. That number is not a coincidence — it reflects the resources, preparation, and experience the government brings to every case.

If you or someone you know is under federal investigation or has been indicted in the Eastern District of Tennessee, the single most important decision you will make is who represents you — and when you make that call. Early intervention by an experienced federal defense attorney can change the trajectory of a case before an indictment is ever handed down.

Joe Hoffer has been involved in more than 150 federal cases as a prosecutor and federal defense attorney across multiple jurisdictions. He currently represents clients in the Eastern District of Tennessee federal courts. He knows what the government is doing — because he spent years doing it.
